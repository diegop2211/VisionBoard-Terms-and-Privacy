FAQs

  • How do I restore my purchase?
    Open the app → Paywall → tap Restore Purchases.
  • What is included in the free vs. paid version?
    See the section below.
  • How do I control access to my Photos?
    iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → VisionBoard 360 → choose Selected Photos or None.

Free vs. One‑time upgrade

Free: create a basic board and try core features.

Unlock Forever (one‑time $9.99): unlimited boards, premium effects, cloud sync across devices, and priority support.

Pricing may vary by region.

Privacy

VisionBoard 360 requests access to your Photo Library so you can select images to build your vision boards. Your photos stay on your device and are only uploaded if you choose to back them up or share. To save exports back to your library, the app also requests permission to add photos.

See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Troubleshooting

  • Purchase not showing? Tap Restore Purchases on the paywall.
  • App crash or bug? Include your device model and iOS version in your message.
  • Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com.
