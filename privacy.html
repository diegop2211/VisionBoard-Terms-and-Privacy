Privacy Policy

Effective October 07, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Diego Pineda (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the VisionBoard mobile application (the “App”).

Summary

  • We collect the minimum data needed to operate the App and process in-app purchases.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • The App does not use third-party advertising or tracking.
  • Questions? Contact pine5510@vandals.uidaho.edu.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App and which optional features you enable, we may process the following:

  • Purchase information (required for premium unlock): transaction receipts, product IDs, and purchase status provided by Apple; purchase verification via a service such as RevenueCat.
  • Diagnostics (to improve stability): crash logs and performance data (e.g., via Sentry or similar).
  • Usage data (optional): basic, aggregate events about which screens/features are used to guide improvements. No ad tracking.
  • User content: images/notes/boards you create. By default these live on your device; if you enable cloud sync/backup (e.g., Firebase or iCloud), your content will be transmitted and stored there under your account.
  • Support communications: any information you send us by email or in-app support.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality (including unlocking purchases and restoring on the same Apple ID).
  • Maintain and improve the App (e.g., diagnose crashes, measure reliability).
  • Communicate about updates, security, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

Sharing

We share information only as needed to run the App or when required by law:

  • Processors/Service providers: Apple (payments), purchase verification (e.g., RevenueCat), diagnostics (e.g., Sentry), and optional cloud storage/sync (e.g., Firebase/iCloud if enabled).
  • Legal and safety: to comply with law, protect rights, or respond to lawful requests.

We do not sell personal information and we do not permit cross-site tracking.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the App and meet legal obligations. If you enable cloud sync, you may delete your content using your cloud provider’s tools. You may request deletion of support records at any time.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your data. To exercise these rights, email pine5510@vandals.uidaho.edu.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

International Transfers

Information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards.

Changes

We may update this Policy. We will change the “Effective” date and, where required, provide notice in the App.

Contact

Contact: pine5510@vandals.uidaho.edu.
